Michael Aguadze
PhD Candidate, Computer Engineering · North Carolina A&T State University

I am a PhD candidate in Computer Engineering at North Carolina A&T State University, where my research focuses on behavioral and motion planning for autonomous vehicles, with an emphasis on high-speed safety using reinforcement learning and simulation frameworks such as Waymax and Metadrive. I previously completed two engineering roles at Amazon — one in Mechatronics (SDE Co-op) and one in Amazon Robotics (Systems Engineering Intern) — building cloud-native services and hardware-software testing platforms.
